## Description

ACIM Daily Minute delivers a daily passage from A Course in Miracles paired with that day's Workbook Lesson, right to your iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch. Each minute is drawn from the Sparkly Edition — the Teddy Poppe / CIMS rendering of the Course — and presented exactly as written, without commentary or interpretation.

Open the app and today's minute is waiting. A short passage to sit with, reflect on, and carry into your day. That's it — one minute, one lesson, every day.

FEATURES

- Today tab: Today's daily minute and Workbook Lesson, refreshed automatically
- 365 Lessons: Browse the full Workbook spine, search by number or keyword, and jump to any lesson
- Listen: Podcast feeds for daily minutes and lessons, plus embedded YouTube audio
- Archive: Calendar-based archive of every past daily minute, searchable by date or text
- Saved: Bookmark any passage for later; swipe to remove
- Reminders: Schedule a daily notification at your preferred time, with a custom chime sound
- Widgets: Small, medium, and large Home Screen widgets showing today's minute at a glance
- Live Activity: Dynamic Island and Lock Screen presence while today's minute is active
- Apple Watch: Today's minute on your wrist, with three complication styles and automatic phone-to-watch sync
- Siri Shortcut: "What's today's reading?" — ask Siri and get the answer instantly
- Deep links: Navigate directly to today's minute, any lesson, any archive date, or your saved list

LINEAGE

The text presented in this app comes from the Sparkly Edition of A Course in Miracles, a rendering by Teddy Poppe published through the Center for Inner Meaning and Significance (CIMS). The Sparkly Edition restores the Course to its original dictation form. This app is an independent companion — it does not represent or claim affiliation with any organization.

PRIVACY

ACIM Daily Minute collects no data whatsoever. There are no analytics, no crash reporters, no ad networks, no user accounts, and no tracking of any kind. Network requests go only to acimdailyminute.org to fetch daily content. All data is stored on your device and never leaves it. Zero third-party SDKs are included — only Apple's built-in frameworks.

This app is offered freely, in the spirit of the Course itself.
